Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information in the Iraq Salary Transparency Portal.

Information Collection

We collect salary and employment-related information that you voluntarily submit through our portal. This includes:

  • Job title and salary information
  • Company name (optional)
  • Industry and location
  • Years of experience
  • Benefits and other compensation details

Anonymity and Data Usage

All submissions are completely anonymous:

  • We do not collect any personally identifiable information
  • Company names are stored separately from salary data
  • Data is aggregated for statistical analysis only
  • Individual submissions cannot be traced back to any person

Data Storage and Security

Your information is stored securely using Firebase services:

  • Data is encrypted both in transit and at rest
  • Access to raw data is restricted to authorized personnel
  • We implement industry-standard security measures
  • Data is stored indefinitely for longitudinal analysis

Purpose of Data Collection

The information you provide helps:

  • Create fair salary benchmarks across Iraqi industries
  • Promote pay transparency and equity
  • Empower job seekers with accurate market information
  • Support evidence-based policy decisions

Your Consent

By submitting information through this portal, you consent to the collection and use of your anonymous data as described in this policy.
